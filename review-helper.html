Planned Features

The primary feature that we are working on is to automatically compile all the different annotations and notes on the paper into a comprehensive coherent review. In terms of implementing this, it would mean, (i) classifying the different types of annotation into 'contributions', 'strengths', 'weaknesses', 'reasons to accept/reject', 'questions to authors', 'typos' and other fields. (ii) for each of those sections, preparing a fluent review from the different annotations.
