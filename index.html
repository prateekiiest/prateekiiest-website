I am a pre-doctoral AI Center Fellow at Microsoft Research India, where I work with Kalika Bali, Monojit Choudhury and Tanuja Ganu. My research interests broadly lie in the intersection of NLP (Natural Language Processing) and HCI (Human Computer Interaction) with an emphasis on their social implications.

Previously, I interned at Carnegie Mellon University with Dave Touretzky and at University College London with Emine Yilmaz and Rishabh Mehrotra. Prior to my time in research, I spent two summers with Google Summer of Code working for Julia Computing and Mozilla.

I did my undergraduate in Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering at BITS Pilani, Goa, India in 2019. Coming fall, I'll be joining University of Washington as a PhD student in Computer Science and Engineering. For more details, please refer to my CV or contact me on .
